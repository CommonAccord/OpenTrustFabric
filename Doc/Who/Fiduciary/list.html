"Fiduciary" is used here in a very specific way. We mean the persons that provide services and products to support transacting. In an ecosystem of contracting, we can think of producers, consumers and Fiduciaries.
Producers include everyone who provides services or products in the real economy. Farmers, artisans, manufacturers, care-givers and entertainers are "producers."
Consumers include persons in their role as the beneficiary of services or products. It includes business as buyers of inputs, and people in their role as citizens and guests.
