An "Infrastructure Fiduciary" is a person who provides or maintains infrastructure for transacting.

The paradigm of an Infrastructure Fiduciary is a telecoms company. It provides wires, fibers, antenna and data centers for communications and storgage of transaction data. Telecoms are usually the backbone and the last mile of data management, connecting mobile devices of users with Data Fiduciaries.

Infrastructure Fiduciaries include the whole logistics chain or warehouses and transport.

Viewed broadly, they include enforcement systems such as police, courts and governments.

As systems become increasingly automated, they will include the providers of security systems, locks, cameras, routing and vehicles.
